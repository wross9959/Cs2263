As with the other htags program, skip over the HTML comment tags. And, just to clarify: a tag is the text between (and including) the < and > characters. Two tags are the same is the text between < and > is the same, e.g. <b> and <b> is the same tag and on the output it should appear just once, but <span lang=EN-US style='font-size:14.0pt> and <span lang=EN-US style='font-size:16.0pt> are different tags and they both should appear on the output.
